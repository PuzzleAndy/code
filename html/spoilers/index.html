Loose Change

Problem: Using only pennies, nickels, dimes, and at least one of each coin, choose 21 coins with a total value of $1.

Solution: 20 nickels would make $1. Exchanging 1 nickel for 5 pennies adds 4 coins. Exchanging 2 nickels for 1 dime removes 1 coin. We need to add 1 coin to go from 20 to 21, so we can add 4 coins and remove 3. This translates to adding 5 pennies and 3 dimes, while removing 7 nickels. After making the exchanges, we have 5 pennies, 3 dimes, 13 nickels.
